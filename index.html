Account Deletion – Rentndeal

Users can delete their account and all associated data directly from the Rentndeal app.

Steps to delete your account

  1. Open the app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Go to Edit Profile
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm deletion

Data deleted

  • User profile
  • Authentication data
  • App usage data stored in Firebase

Retention

No data is retained after account deletion.

If you have any issues deleting your account, contact us at support@rentndeal.com.
